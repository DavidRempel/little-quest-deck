Rules

  • Choose an attack type: Match, Run, Colour, or Set.
  • Invalid selections cannot attack. Extra off-pattern cards do not help.
  • Score = combo value + card bonus + abilities + monster/relic effects.
  • Discard selected cards once per monster for free; extra discards cost 1 HP. Tricky monsters charge immediately.
  • Near misses hurt less, perfect hits charge power, and big overkills can improve treasure.
