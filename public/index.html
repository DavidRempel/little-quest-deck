Rules
  • Attack with selected cards. The game automatically chooses the highest-scoring valid combo: Match, Sequence, or Flush. The score panel shows every part of the total. The Attack button turns green when the selected attack will defeat the enemy, red when it only deals partial damage.
  • Starting hand size is 5. Hand size can rise to a max of 7.
  • Match scores: Pair 9, Three-kind 27, Four-kind 63. Match groups stack, so two pair = 18 and full house = 36.
  • Sequence scores: 3/4/5/6/7 cards score 15/35/68/120/192.
  • Flush scores: 3/4/5/6/7 cards score 12/28/55/96/154. A straight flush stacks its Sequence and Flush scores.
  • Valid combos: Match = one or more matching-number groups, including pair, two pair, three/four of a kind, or full house; unmatched selected cards add no Match score. Sequence = 3+ consecutive numbers. Flush = 3+ same colour.
  • Enemy weakness is one broad family: Match, Sequence, or Flush. Matching it multiplies the combo's base score by 1.5 before equipment bonuses. Matching selected cards glow and jiggle so the bonus is hard to miss. Combo bonuses stack; a straight flush gets both Sequence and Flush.
  • Enemies have HP. If your attack is less than their total HP, it still knocks their HP down, raises Temper by 1, then they hit back.
  • Temper: each enemy starts at 0 Temper. Partial attacks and emergency swaps raise Temper before retaliation, up to 3. Each Temper adds +1 to that enemy's attack. Temper resets when a new enemy appears.
  • Enemies 5, 10, and 15 are bosses. Enemy 16 is the final boss: -1 effective hand size, Crown Cracks damage applied, win prize on defeat, then you can keep playing endless mode.
  • Regroup: swap exactly one selected card and take a half-strength hit. You get one Regroup charge for each five-encounter chapter; it recharges after defeating a boss. If the charge is spent and your hand has no valid attack at all, an Emergency Swap is allowed but raises Temper and takes a full-strength hit.
  • Clear Selection deselects your current cards without spending a turn.
  • After a non-lethal attack, replace every card played. Defeating an enemy fully refills your hand for the next encounter.
  • Clean Victory: defeat with 0-3 excess damage to gain +2 gold. Before the final boss, each Clean Victory also removes 2 HP from its crown, capped at 12 HP total. Boss Clean Victories also heal 1 HP.
  • Each run has a seed in the page URL. Restart the same seed to replay identical cards, enemies, and rewards, or start a new seed for a fresh run.
  • Equipment slots start with basic no-bonus gear. Normal monsters have a 15% chance to drop equipment; boss shops remain guaranteed. New gear is labelled as a clear upgrade, clear downgrade, or trade-off against the equipped item before you choose.
  • Deck editing rewards — adding or removing cards — are not in this build yet.
