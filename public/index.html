Little Quest Deck

A stripped-back card-combo prototype. Fight one enemy at a time. Each turn, choose one of two actions: Attack with selected cards, or Discard X → Draw X to cycle cards without increasing hand size. Cycling lets the enemy hit you for HP. Attacks refill your hand back toward your current hand size.

Rules

  • Attack with selected cards. The game automatically chooses the highest-scoring valid combo: Match, Straight, Flush, Straight Flush, or Set. The Attack button turns green when the selected attack beats the enemy target, red when it is short.
  • Starting hand size is 5. Future buffs can raise that hand size.
  • Score math: selected card count × combo-size multiplier. Weakness doubles the result. Straight Flush gets an extra ×2.
  • Multipliers: 2 cards ×2, 3 cards ×4, 4 cards ×8, 5 cards ×16.
  • Valid combos: Match = 2+ same number; Straight = 3+ consecutive numbers; Flush = 2+ same colour; Straight Flush = 3+ consecutive cards of one colour; Set = 3+ cards with all different numbers and all different colours.
  • Every 5th enemy is a boss. Defeating a boss gives +1 hand size.
  • Discard X → Draw X discards as many selected cards as you want, then draws the same number back, so your hand does not grow.
  • After an attack, played cards are replaced up to your current hand size.
  • Enemy attacks deal HP damage only in this build.
  • No treasures, rewards, relics, traits, enemy modifiers, or attack-type buttons in this build.
