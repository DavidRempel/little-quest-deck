Rules
  • Attack with selected cards. Match, Sequence, and Flush use their combo scores. If the selected cards make no combo, they become a Basic Hit for 1 damage per card. The score panel shows every part of the total. Lethal cards get a separate Finisher animation that stacks with the weakness effect.
  • Starting hand size is 5. Hand size can rise to a max of 7.
  • Match scores: Pair 9, Three-kind 27, Four-kind 63. Match groups stack, so two pair = 18 and full house = 36.
  • Sequence scores: 3/4/5/6/7 cards score 15/35/68/120/192.
  • Flush scores: 3/4/5/6/7 cards score 12/28/55/96/154. A straight flush stacks its Sequence and Flush scores.
  • Combos: Match = one or more matching-number groups, including pair, two pair, three/four of a kind, or full house; unmatched selected cards add no Match score. Sequence = 3+ consecutive numbers. Flush = 3+ same colour. A non-combo selection is always a valid Basic Hit.
  • Enemy weakness is one broad family: Match, Sequence, or Flush. Matching it multiplies the combo's base score by 1.5 before equipment bonuses. Matching selected cards glow and jiggle so the bonus is hard to miss. Combo bonuses stack; a straight flush gets both Sequence and Flush.
  • Enemies have HP. If your attack is less than their total HP, it still knocks their HP down, raises Temper by 1, then they hit back.
  • Temper: each enemy starts at 0 Temper. Partial attacks raise Temper before retaliation, up to 3. Each Temper adds +1 to that enemy's attack. Temper resets when a new enemy appears.
  • Enemies 5, 10, and 15 are bosses. Enemy 16 is the final boss: -1 effective hand size, Crown Cracks damage applied, win prize on defeat, then you can keep playing endless mode.
  • Swap Bank / Regroup: start with 1 Swap token. Spend 1 to replace exactly one selected card and take a half-strength hit. Defeating an enemy with its weakness earns +1 Swap token, up to 9, so tokens can be saved for bosses.
  • Clear Selection deselects your current cards without spending a turn.
  • After a non-lethal attack, replace every card played. Defeating an enemy fully refills your hand for the next encounter.
  • Victory bonuses: 0-3 excess damage is a Clean Victory worth +2 gold; before the final boss it also cracks 2 crown HP, capped at 12, and boss Clean Victories heal 1 HP. A Big Overkill of 4+ excess damage earns +1 gold and raises a normal monster's equipment chance from 15% to 30%. Weakness Finishes also earn a Swap token.
  • Each run has a seed in the page URL. Restart the same seed to replay identical cards, enemies, and rewards, or start a new seed for a fresh run.
  • Equipment slots start with basic no-bonus gear. Normal monsters have a 15% base equipment chance; boss shops are guaranteed. New gear is labelled as a clear upgrade, clear downgrade, or trade-off. The Bigger Backpack has its own 🎒 card and clearly shows Hand +1.
  • Shop: buying an item marks only that card sold; the remaining shelf stays put. Rerolling the whole shelf costs 4 gold, then 6, 8, and so on during that shop visit.
  • Deck editing rewards — adding or removing cards — are not in this build yet.
